How It Works

Virtual Host is your personal assistant when you leave the house and can answer any question yours guests may have.

Simply answer questions your guests could possibly have about your space, and your virtual host will take over from there.

Virtual Host answers your guests' questions the same way you would!

  • Create an Account

    Sign up here

  • Answer Questions

    Answer questions the way you'd like your Virtual Host to reply to them. You can choose to answer questions we predicted your guests may have, or create and answer your own questions about any special amenities your space has.

  • Ditch your guests

    Let your Virtual Host take care of the rest! Whether you are busy hosting an event and would like a co-host, or can't be present when you are AirBnBing your space, Virtual Host is here to help you.
